Overview

Turning 2 years of data into a forward-looking strategy

RedTape's operations teams were making inventory and logistics decisions based on intuition and lagging reports. The goal was to build a 2-year historical sales dashboard capable of exposing seasonal trends, regional patterns, and payment behaviour — and use that intelligence to drive concrete operational improvements.

The project spanned over 1 million transaction records, covering multiple SKUs, geographies, and payment modes across a 24-month window.

Key Findings

What the 2-year view revealed

Seasonal Demand Peaks

October and February consistently showed 2.4x average order volume — driven by festive season and end-of-winter sale cycles, enabling more accurate pre-stocking.

Regional RTO Patterns

Tier-3 cities contributed 62% of total RTO volume. COD prevalence in these regions was the primary driver — targeted prepaid incentives were modelled per zone.

Payment Mode Impact

Prepaid orders returned at 3.2% vs 22% for COD. The revenue leakage from COD-related returns was quantified and communicated to pricing strategy teams.

Dashboard Design

How the Power BI solution was structured

The dashboard was structured across four interconnected report pages: Sales Overview (volume, revenue, trend), Payment Mode Analysis (prepaid vs COD by region and category), RTO Deep Dive (return rates by pincode, product, and time), and Forecasting View (30/60/90-day projections using trend decomposition).

Drill-through navigation allowed warehouse and marketing leads to move from company-level aggregates to individual SKU performance without leaving the report. Scheduled refresh ensured live-accurate data across stakeholders.

Three KPIs moved simultaneously

Prepaid adoption rose 40%, reducing the financial exposure from returns. RTO fell 15%, cutting reverse logistics costs. Inventory accuracy improved 17%, reducing both overstock and stockout incidents. These were driven by data-backed changes to logistics routing, prepaid discount targeting, and demand forecasting cycles.
